Lentel Spaghetti Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 4 tbsp oil
  • 1 1/2 cups red split lentals
  • 1 whole chilli
  • 900 mL water
  • 2 tsp beef stock powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1 420g tomato can
  • 170g tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 packet of spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese

Method

  1. Cook onion and garlic in oil for 5 mins.
  2. Add lentels, diced chilli. Season well with salt and pepper, then add the water.
  3. Simmer for 20 mins.
  4. Add all of the remaining ingredients except the pasta and cheese, then simmer for a further 10-15 mins.
  5. Serve the sauce over boiled spaghetti with grated cheese on top.
